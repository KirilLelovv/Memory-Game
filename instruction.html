Instruction on how to play the game

The memory game is a game where you need to match the pictures




Game Points:

Game Pairs One Pair Two Pair Three Pair Four Pair Five Pair Six Pair
Game Points 25 points 50 points 75 points 100 points 125 points 150 points



Instructions on how to play the game:


  • Press or click on one of the squares to revial the picture
  • Be careful at the time because it can ran out
  • Once you click on the square the picture will flip and revial itself
  • Try clicking on the other squares to revial the other pictures
  • Once you flip two squares try matching the same picture to be correct and earn points
  • Match as many as you can
  • Once you match them all together you win at the game
